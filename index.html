About

WildCats Voices is your space to speak up and bring ideas to life! Whether it's a new spirit day, a fundraiser, or a homecoming theme, just submit your idea and we’ll bring it to the table during SCA meetings.

The Student Council is here to represent YOU, so don’t hold back — your voice matters!
